# How to choose validation library


## Validators by features

1. Type casting
    1. Cerberus
    1. DjBurger
    1. WTForms
1. Optional fields
1. Required fields
    1. Cerberus
    1. DjangoRESTFramework
    1. DjBurger
    1. Marshmallow
    1. PySchemes
    1. WTForms
1. Drop unknown fields
    1. Cerberus
    1. DjBurger
    1. WTForms
1. Django querysets support
1. Django models support
    1. DjangoRESTFramework
    1. Marshmallow


## Validators by recommended validation type

1. Pre-validation
    1. Cerberus
    1. DjBurger
    1. WTForms
1. Post-validation
    1. DjangoRESTFramework
    1. Marshmallow
    1. PySchemes (doesn't support Django models)
